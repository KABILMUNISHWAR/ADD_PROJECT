My Trip to Athirappilly Waterfalls

About Athirappilly Waterfalls

Athirappilly Waterfalls, located in the Thrissur district of Kerala, India, is often referred to as the "Niagara of India." It is one of the most popular tourist destinations in Kerala, drawing visitors for its stunning beauty and serene environment.

My Experience

The moment I arrived at Athirappilly, I was mesmerized by the sight of the cascading water. The falls stand tall at around 80 feet and the surrounding lush green forest adds to the charm of the place. The sound of the water crashing down is both calming and powerful, offering a truly unique experience.

Things to Do Around Athirappilly

  • Take a walk along the paths that surround the waterfall.
  • Visit the nearby Vazhachal Waterfalls, which are only a short drive away.
  • Explore the wildlife sanctuary and spot some of the diverse flora and fauna of the region.
  • Enjoy a peaceful boat ride along the river.

Travel Tips

Here are some tips to make your visit to Athirappilly Waterfalls more enjoyable:

  • Wear comfortable shoes, as there are some uphill walks and rocky paths around the falls.
  • Carry water and snacks, as food options may be limited in the area.
  • Try to visit early in the morning to avoid the crowds and to enjoy the peaceful atmosphere.
  • Be careful near the water as it can get slippery, especially after the rains.
