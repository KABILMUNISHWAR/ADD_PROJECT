Explore Destinations

Kerala

Experience the serene backwaters, lush greenery, and vibrant culture of "God's Own Country." Must-visit attractions include Munnar, Alleppey, and the stunning Athirapally Waterfalls.

Rajasthan

Discover the royal heritage of Rajasthan with its majestic forts, palaces, and golden deserts. Highlights include Jaipur, Jodhpur, and the iconic Amber Fort.

Goa

Relax on the sun-kissed beaches of Goa, explore vibrant markets, and enjoy the lively nightlife. Perfect for beach lovers and adventure seekers alike.

Leh Ladakh

Embark on a journey to the breathtaking landscapes of Leh Ladakh. Known for its rugged mountains and tranquil monasteries, it's a paradise for adventure enthusiasts.

Andaman Islands

Dive into the crystal-clear waters of the Andaman Islands. Explore pristine beaches, coral reefs, and historic sites like Cellular Jail.

Darjeeling

Visit the "Queen of the Hills" and enjoy stunning views of the Kanchenjunga range. Famous for its tea plantations and the charming toy train.
