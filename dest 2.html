My Visit to Amber Fort

About Amber Fort

Amber Fort (also known as Amer Fort) is located in Jaipur, Rajasthan. This magnificent fort, built in the 16th century by Raja Man Singh, is known for its grand architecture, stunning courtyards, and historical significance. The fort is a UNESCO World Heritage Site and one of the most popular tourist attractions in India.

My Experience at Amber Fort

Visiting Amber Fort was like stepping into a page of history. As I walked through the grand gates and past the massive walls, I was amazed by the architectural beauty. The fort is a perfect blend of Hindu and Mughal architecture, with intricate carvings, ornate decorations, and expansive courtyards. The panoramic views of the surrounding hills and the Maota Lake were breathtaking.

The highlight of my visit was the Sheesh Mahal (Mirror Palace), which is an absolute marvel. The entire palace is adorned with mirrors that create a mesmerizing effect, especially when lit. The sound and light show in the evening adds another layer of magic to the experience.

Things to Do at Amber Fort

  • Take a ride on the elephant to the main entrance (it's a popular tradition!).
  • Explore the Sheesh Mahal (Mirror Palace), which is one of the most beautiful parts of the fort.
  • Visit the Jaleb Chowk, the main courtyard of the fort, which is surrounded by beautiful structures.
  • Enjoy the stunning views of Maota Lake from the fort walls.
  • Take a guided tour to learn about the history and significance of the fort.
